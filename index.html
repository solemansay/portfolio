Expertise

I focus on crafting websites with an elegant user interface that people of all ages can use.

- My first step on any project no matter the size or ambition is to sit down and sudo code it with a pen and paper. I think about how I want the basic site to function and what it is I am setting out to accomplish, before even typing a line of code.

  • Product Design

    Given an idea, I excel at visualizing a modern website design that will satisfy the eye and provide a smooth and easy user experience while navigating the interface.

  • Animations

    Something I pride myself in doing is adding sublte yet noticable, clean animations to my projects. I am well versed in CSS as well as SCSS to create seamless animations on load, scroll, hover or click.

My first and foremost passion is in Frontend Development.
